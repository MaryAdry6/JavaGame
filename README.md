#### Description

Magic Heart is a 2D top-down, single-player action-adventure hack-and-slash game. Players control the protagonist, Aileen, a bride on a mission to save her groom, Dante, the prince of the realm and guardian of the "Magic Heart"—the epicenter of magic in this universe.



#### The Narrative Arc

The story begins at the couple's wedding ceremony, which is interrupted by the game’s antagonist: Azula (Aileen's jealous twin sister). Azula steals the prince's "heart," shatters it into four distinct stones, and scatters them across the land.

Fortunately, the godmother of the wedding, a benevolent fairy named Eartha, manages to save one of the fragments: the Forest Stone. With this stone, Aileen gains the ability to fight nature-based enemies as she embarks on the first level. At the end of this stage, she faces the first boss, a formidable Gorilla, whose defeat yields the Mountain Stone.



#### Gameplay Mechanics \& Progression

The game utilizes an elemental progression system where each acquired stone provides an advantage in the subsequent area:

* Level 2 (The Mountain): Introduces new mountain-themed enemies that are more easily defeated using the stone obtained from the first boss. However, players will still encounter forest enemies, introducing a swapping mechanic where the player must switch stones in real-time based on the enemy type encountered.
* The Second Boss: A Stone Golem guards the end of this level. Defeating it grants the third fragment, the Volcano Stone.
* The Final Level: Set inside a volcano, this stage challenges the player to battle all three types of enemies simultaneously.



#### The Climax

The final confrontation is against Azula herself. Upon her defeat, Aileen recovers the final stone, allowing her to restore the Magic Heart and save the Prince. Following a circular narrative structure, the game concludes where it began: with the wedding of Aileen and Dante—this time, proceeding without incident.
